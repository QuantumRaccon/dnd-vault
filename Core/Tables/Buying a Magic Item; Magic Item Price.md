### Magic Item Price
---
|Rarity|Asking Price* |
|-------|---------|
|Common|**(1d6 + 1) × 10** gp|
|Uncommon|**1d6 × 100** gp|
|Rare|**2d10 × 1,000** gp|
|Very rare|**(1d4 + 1) × 10,000** gp|
|Legendary|**2d6 × 25,000** gp|
[^1] 

[^1]: Halved for a consumable item like a potion or scroll
